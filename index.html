INSTRUCTIONS

The objective of the game is to avoid touching obstacles of different color.

You can make the ball jump by clicking inside the playing area.

The game starts AUTOMATICALLY 1sec after you click on 'EASY' or 'HARD'.

You lose the moment you touch an obstacle of different color.

PRO TIP: Don't try 'HARD' unless you've mastered 'EASY'.
